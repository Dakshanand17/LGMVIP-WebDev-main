About SCA

The Shahjahanpur Cricket Assosiation in Shahjahanpur (SCA) is the state governing body for cricket in Shahjahanpur, Affliated to Uttar Pradesh Cricket Assosiation (UPCA).It is a consortium of ditrict cricket association and their representatives.We organize Cricket and its related events every year in Shahjahanpur.
